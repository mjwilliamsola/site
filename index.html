About

Omega Leadership Academy of Denver is a flagship program of the  Holmes Omega Scholarship Foundation and Chi Phi Chapter of Omega Psi Phi Fraternity, Inc.  

The program is focused on helping Denver-area young men of middle and high school age grow and develop their leadership talents in every phase of human endeavor. Our program is designed to be engaging, fun and educational with a focus on short and long-term success for every member.  

Vision and Mission



Vision: A future network of intelligent and strong black leaders with a foundation in the Denver-metro area

Mission: Providing Denver-area boys of color with the tools to be tomorrows leaders





Requirements

 Boys, ages 12-18 or in grades 6th to 12th

 Member commitment to excellence in scholastic and community endeavors

 Member and parent/guardian commitment to the program until graduation (2-3 events a month during the school year and limited activity during the summer



Exposure to career pathways is a key component of what we do.

We tour workplaces on a regular basis, tech companies, law firms, video production facilities, medical facilities and more every year. 

We visit and tour college campuses every year to expose and normalize higher education as an attainable goal.

We also visit technical and trade schools so members have a well-rounded view of what post-secondary education can be.   

OLA members participate in regular volunteer and service-learning activities in the community.

We teach young men the importance of being pillars in our communities by giving back for a lifetime.  

From financial literacy to sex ed to public speaking and more, we create opportunities for members to acquire the skills needed to build a successful life.

We bring in subject matter experts and use engaging lesson plans to ensure members have fun while learning. 

The OLA Hop team is a step team in the traditional style of Omega Psi Phi Fraternity, Inc.

“Hop” involves elements of dance, singing, and athleticism.
